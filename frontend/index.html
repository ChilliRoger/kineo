02

THE MECHANISM

Real-time voice + vision agent. Kineo understands the problem, scores the risk,
and wins the customer back—all in under 60 seconds.

INPUT

LISTEN & WATCH

Customer starts a live session. Gemini Live API processes voice tone and video feed simultaneously to identify product damage (Wrong Item / Broken) and sentiment.

PROCESS

RISK SCORING

Kineo pulls order history from Firestore. It computes a churn risk score based on frustration level, LTV (Lifetime Value), and return frequency.

ACTION

THE WIN-BACK

Before the app closes, Kineo speaks a personalized offer: Free Replacement, Store Credit, or Discount. Fully automated on Cloud Run.
