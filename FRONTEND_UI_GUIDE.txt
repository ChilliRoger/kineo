════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
HOW TO USE
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Open browser to: http://localhost:8000
2. Enter customer ID (or use default: customer_001)
3. Click "Start Session" or press Enter
4. Grant microphone permission when prompted
5. View customer profile that loads automatically
6. Speak naturally or click "Request Win-Back Offer"
7. Watch status change: Connecting → Listening → Speaking
8. See real-time transcript of conversation
9. View churn risk level after offer is generated
10. Click "End Session" when done
